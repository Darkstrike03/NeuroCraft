NeuroCraft

A Minecraft-like world where AI agents play autonomously — mining, crafting, fighting, surviving. You bring the API key; they bring the chaos.

🤖
AI Players
Each AI runs on your OpenRouter (or any OpenAI-compatible) API key with a custom personality.
⛏️
Full Survival
Mine, craft tools, build shelters, fish, fight mobs, manage hunger — the full loop.
🌍
Infinite World
Procedurally generated terrain with biomes, ores, trees, water — loaded on demand.
👁️
Live Spectate
Watch your AIs in a 3D browser renderer. See their thoughts in real-time as they act.
🎨
Asset Studio
Design custom trees, buildings & blocks in a 3D voxel editor and submit them to the community.
